About the Program

This bachelor program combines mathematics, data science, physics, and statistics to solve real-world problems.

Program Structure

1st Year

Fall Semester: Discrete Mathematics, Programming, Technology & Society

Spring Semester: Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry, Databases

2nd Year

Fall Semester: Advanced Mathematics, Modern Physics

Spring Semester: Advanced Mathematics, Thermodynamics, Symmetry & Structures

3rd Year

Fall Semester: Real Analysis, Data Science Introduction

Spring Semester: Bachelor Thesis
